About the artist

Lithops Marmoratta is a young artist designing some really creative content. Based in Paris, his workshop gathers all of his creations, born from the use of very different techniques on paper or on digital devices. His work is easily recognizable as it is often inspired from imaginary worlds or cultural content everyone is familiar with.
